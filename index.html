Run! Human! Run!



Humans have spread and consumed unchecked for far too long.
They thought no one was watching...Well they were!

Take control of every single member of the human race
and try to survive in countless trials to prove your species worthy
of living on the planet earth. You play or you die!



  • Run, jump, Climb, Swing, Glide and Winch to get to the end of each course.
  • Replay levels time limit free to collect cash items and unlock secret courses.
  • 25+ levels so far and a lot more on the way
  • 2 episodes currently with more coming
  • Course times are displayed in microseconds.
  • It's possible to beat your own time by 0.000001th of a second
  • In game commentary from Frunk Magoop and Paul Destroyer the celestial heads
  • Use cash to upgrade the human race in any way you want
    with Skill points that can be rearranged anytime to allow full control of the humans abilites.
  • Human profiles are generated at random from a list of over
    100+ names, jobs,home towns and ages. Each time you die you
    get a new humans with new details.
  • Human profiles names and details can be carried over
    between rounds. If you are good enough you can take the
    same prisoner through the entire game.
  • Secret rounds that give the players a respite from running
    and jumping.
  • Each episode is only complete when the total best time of
    all courses is less than the required time to proceed.
  • Beating your best times is key to unlocking the next episode.
  • Each stage has a developer time that awards extra cash if beaten
  • Find and unlock extra feats like, double jump and slow down time
  • Each skill can be multiplied several times, allowing for quadrupal jump and super slow Mo
  • Find audio tapes to unlock the raw unedited audio of the actors recording thier lines
  • Works on PC, Mac and Linux
  • Offline play with free updates and dlc.
  • No lootboxes or data harvesting (As if I needed to say this>
  • Even more features in the works.
