Run! Human! Run!

Humans have spread and consumed unchecked for far too long.
They thought no one was watching...Well they were!

Take control of every single member of the human race
and try to survive in countless trials to prove your species worthy
of living on the planet earth. You play or you die!

Run, Climb, Swing, Glide and Winch to get to the end of each course. Replay levels time limit free to collect cash items and unlock secret courses.
25+ levels so far and more planned
2 episodes currently with more coming
Use cash to upgrade the human race in any way you want. With Skillpoints that can be rearranged anytime to allow full control of the humans abilites.
Human profiles are generated at random from a list of over 100+ names, jobs,home towns and ages. Each time you die you get a new humans with new details.
Human profiles names and details can be carried over between rounds. If you are good enough you can take the same prisoner through the entire game.
Secret rounds that give the players a respite from running and jumping.
Each episode is only complete when the total best time of all levels is less than the required time to proceed. Beating your best times is key to unlocking the next episode.
